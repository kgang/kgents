# Taste and Design Interview

Purpose: gather Kent's aesthetic and interaction preferences to finalize the frontend redesign.

Please answer as much as you can. Short answers are fine.

---

## 1) Overall vibe

1. Pick 2-3 adjectives for the UI atmosphere (examples: austere, lush, clinical, ritual, playful, precise, contemplative, kinetic, mythic).
2. Do you want the UI to feel more like a cockpit, a studio, a library, a laboratory, or a cathedral?
3. When you are deep in work, what mood do you want the interface to create?

---

## 2) Typography and language

4. Do you prefer serif or sans for main body text?
5. How dense should text feel? (tight, normal, spacious)
6. Do you want headings to feel formal or experimental?
7. Do you prefer terse labels or poetic labels?

---

## 3) Color and material

8. Are there any colors you want to avoid?
9. Do you want a bright canvas or a muted canvas?
10. Should backgrounds be flat or textured?
11. Do you prefer sharp geometric shapes or organic shapes?

---

## 4) Motion and behavior

12. How much motion is acceptable? (none, minimal, moderate, expressive)
13. Are you ok with animated graph edges and transitions?
14. Should the UI feel calm and stable or alive and breathing?

---

## 5) Hierarchy and density

15. Do you want maximal information density or more whitespace?
16. How many panels should be visible by default? (1, 2, 3+)
17. Should the graph always be visible, or only on demand?

---

## 6) References and anti-references

18. Any products or interfaces you love and want to echo?
19. Any interfaces you strongly dislike?
20. Any specific visual metaphors you want to avoid?

---

## 7) Accessibility and comfort

21. Do you need high contrast by default?
22. Do you prefer large text or compact text?
23. Any color vision concerns?

---

## 8) Personal ritual

24. Describe a perfect first 2 minutes in the app.
25. Describe a perfect last 2 minutes before you stop for the day.
