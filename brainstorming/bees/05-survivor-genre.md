# Survivor/Roguelike Genre Mechanics

> 50 mechanics borrowed from Vampire Survivors, Brotato, and similar games

---

## Progression Systems

### Meta-Progression
**1. Hive Upgrades** - Between runs, spend honey/pollen for permanent buffs:
- "Reinforced Wax Cells" (+5% max health)
- "Royal Jelly Reserve" (+10% damage)
- "Efficient Foraging" (+8% XP gain)
- Like VS PowerUp system

**2. Queen's Favor (Achievements)** - Challenges unlock new bee types:
- "Survive 10 minutes with no upgrades" → Solitary Bee
- "Kill 1000 wasps" → Killer Bee variant

**3. Pollination Log (Codex)** - Track all flowers, enemies, synergies. Each entry = small permanent bonus. Complete categories = special unlockables.

**4. Seasonal Cycles (Ascension)** - After completing runs, unlock harder seasons:
- Spring (normal) → Summer (faster) → Autumn (scarcity) → Winter (hazards)
- Stacking modifiers for ultimate challenge

**5. Bee Lineage (Rogue Legacy)** - Death passes one trait to next bee:
- "Daughter of the Bombardier" = +1 projectile, -10% speed

## Run Modifiers

### Arcana-Style "Flower Cards"
**6. 22 unlockable modifier cards:**
- "The Orchid" (0): All attacks leave poison trails
- "The Sunflower" (X): Power in lit areas, weaken in shadows
- "The Corpse Flower" (XIII): Death explosions attract more enemies

### Curse System
**7. Curse of the Pesticide** - Voluntary difficulty modifiers:
- "Neonicotinoid Exposure" (-20% attack speed, +50% honey)
- "Colony Collapse" (2x respawn, +75% XP)

**8. Hyper/Hurry Modes:**
- "Frenzied Swarm" = 65% faster everything
- "Migration Mode" = start at minute 10 difficulty
- "Endless Bloom" = no time limits

**9. Hazard Modifiers (Deep Rock style):**
- "Smoke Warning" (periodic vision reduction)
- "Drought Conditions" (50% fewer health pickups)
- "Invasive Species" (new enemy types)

**10. Weekly Challenge** - Fixed modifiers, global leaderboard, unique rewards.

## Weapon/Ability Types

### Attack Patterns
**11. Stinger Barrage** - Straight line projectiles. Evolves with "Venom Sac" → "Toxic Needles" (pierce + poison).

**12. Pollen Cloud** - Expanding AoE slow+damage. Evolves with "Wind Rider" → "Allergen Storm" (massive area).

**13. Waggle Dance** - Summons worker bees. Evolves with "Hive Mind" → "Royal Guard" (soldier bees).

**14. Propolis Shield** - Reflects projectiles. Evolves with "Tree Sap" → "Amber Prison" (freeze enemies).

**15. Buzz Saw** - Orbiting damage. Evolves with "Formation Flying" → "Tornado Swarm" (massive vortex).

**16. Nectar Beam** - Continuous channel. Evolves with "Sugar Rush" → "Honey Laser" (double width + burn).

**17. Flower Bomb** - Deployable mines. Evolves with "Seed Scatter" → "Garden of Doom" (chain explosions).

**18. Six-Weapon Slots (Brotato)** - Mix melee stingers, ranged pollen, summoned allies for emergent synergies.

## Synergy/Evolution

### Pollination Synergies
**19. Weapon + Passive = Evolution:**
- Stinger Barrage + Venom Sac = Toxic Needles
- Buzz Saw + Royal Jelly = Golden Swarm (gold damage + money)
- Pollen Cloud + Fire = "Smoke Screen" (blind + burn)
- Nectar Beam + Ice = "Crystallized Honey" (freeze beam)

**20. Elemental Paths (20 Minutes Till Dawn style):**
- Fire: Burning, explosions
- Ice: Freeze, slow, shatter
- Electric: Chain lightning
- Poison: DOT stacking, death explosions

**21. Multi-Crit Stacking** - Crit chance >100% = multiple crits per hit. "Triple Sting Critical" at 150%.

**22. Synergy Preview** - Pause menu shows all possible synergies and requirements.

## Passive Items

### Six Passive Slots
**23. Passive system:**
- "Extra Wings" (+movement speed)
- "Compound Eyes" (+pickup radius/magnet)
- "Reinforced Exoskeleton" (+armor)
- "Pheromone Glands" (+summon damage)
- "Honey Stomach" (+health regen)
- "Antenna Sensitivity" (+XP gain)

**24. Stacking** - Each passive levels 5 times. "Extra Wings" = +8% per level, max +40%.

**25. Trade-offs (Brotato style):**
- "Aggressive Foraging" (+25% damage, -15% health)
- "Heavy Pollen Load" (+40% area, -20% speed)

## Enemy Variety

### Wasp Hierarchy
**26. Drone Wasps** - Basic swarmers, low HP, high numbers
**27. Soldier Wasps** - Tanky, charge attacks
**28. Paper Wasp Architects** - Create spawning nests
**29. Hornet Elites** - Fast, high damage, relentless chase
**30. Queen Wasp Boss** - Spawns minions, area attacks, multiple phases

### Predator Types
**31. Spiders** - Web traps, ambush from edges
**32. Dragonflies** - Fast aerial, strafe attacks
**33. Praying Mantis** - High damage ambush, camouflage
**34. Birds** - Swooping attacks, can grab player

### Environmental
**35. Pesticide Clouds** - Damage zones drifting across
**36. Rain Puddles** - Slow movement, drowning damage
**37. Human Hands** - Massive boss-like swatting

### Events
**38. Swarm Events:**
- "Wasp Migration" = 50 wasps sweeping screen
- "Mantis Ambush" = encircled by high-HP enemies

**39. The Beekeeper (Final Boss)** - Unkillable at 30min like The Reaper. Smokes arena, nets player.

## Map/Stage Design

### Biomes
**40. Biome variety:**
- Meadow: Open, moderate difficulty
- Orchard: Fruit trees, vertical movement
- Greenhouse: Enclosed, pesticide hazards
- Urban Garden: Human interference
- Wild Forest: Dense, predator-heavy
- Hive Defense: Tower-defense style

### Hazards
**41. Environmental hazards:**
- Sprinkler zones (periodic water damage)
- Lawnmower paths (moving instant death)
- Venus flytraps (trap zones)
- Sun/shade mechanics

### Secrets
**42. Hidden content:**
- Rare flowers with powerful relics
- Paths behind bushes
- Secret bosses guarding evolutions

**43. Destructible Environment** - Break stems for resources, destroy nests, open seed pods.

## Additional Mechanics

**50. Weapon Merging (Brotato)** - Combine two tier-1 → one tier-2 with improved stats.

**51. Wave Structure (Brotato)** - 20-90 second waves with shop breaks. Strategic decision points.

**52. Colony Mode (Co-op)** - 2-4 bees defend hive together. Shared resources, combined synergies.

**53. Adventure Mode** - Linear story with persistent upgrades between zones (like VS Adventure).

**54. Random Events:**
- "Rare Flower Bloom" (bonus loot)
- "Predator Invasion" (bonus XP)
- "Pollination Rush" (speed boost for collection)
