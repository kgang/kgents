# Gameplay Mechanics Brainstorm

> 50 core mechanics, abilities, and systems for Hornet Siege

---

## Player Abilities

### Core Movement

**1. Vespa Dash** - Short burst leaving damaging pheromone trail. Enemies crossing take DoT. Rewards aggressive positioning.

**2. Hover Brake** - Instant stop + brief invulnerability. "Threading the needle" between swarm lines.

**3. Divebomb** - Hold to charge, release to plummet. Longer charge = higher requirement. Vertical space as resource.

**4. Draft Riding** - Speed boost behind fast enemies (cycling drafting). Inverts chase dynamic.

**5. Sideslip** - Double-tap lateral dodge phasing through projectiles. Stamina cost.

### Attack Types

**6. Mandible Slash** - Wide arc melee. "Scissor" upgrade: enemies between two slashes take critical damage.

**7. Stinger Strike** - Precise forward lunge. High damage, narrow hitbox, pierces enemies.

**8. Venom Spit** - Ranged projectile with stacking debuff. Three stacks = paralysis.

**9. Wing Buffet** - AoE knockback. Low damage, creates breathing room. "Vacuum" variant pulls first.

**10. Pheromone Bomb** - Delayed explosive attracting enemies before detonation. Crowd control puzzle.

### Defensive Moves

**11. Carapace Harden** - Damage reduction reflecting damage back. Punishes mindless swarming.

**12. Decoy Molt** - Shed exoskeleton attracting attention + brief invisibility.

**13. Vibration Counter** - Perfect parry stunning all nearby enemies. High skill ceiling.

**14. Emergency Evasion** - Below 20% health: free teleport to safety. 60s cooldown. "Clutch save."

### Ultimates

**15. Murder Hornet Mode** - 10s: 3x size, armor, one-shot attacks. Power fantasy payoff.

**16. Pheromone Storm** - Mark all visible enemies. 3s later: damage proportional to marks. Rewards letting swarm build.

**17. Queen's Wrath** - Summon 5 allied hornets for 15s inheriting your upgrades.

**18. Time Dilation Buzz** - Wings slow time for everyone except you. 5s of thinking room.

## Upgrade Trees

### Offensive

**19. Serrated Mandibles** - Melee causes bleeding (stacks 5x). Synergizes with attack speed.

**20. Venomous Lineage** - All attacks apply venom. Damage scales with enemy max HP.

**21. Execution Protocol** - +50% damage to enemies below 25% health. Finisher playstyle.

**22. Berserker Instinct** - +5% damage per nearby enemy (stacks to 50%). Dangerous positioning.

### Defensive

**23. Regenerative Chitin** - Slow health regen. Rate increases when not taking damage.

**24. Damage Threshold** - Ignore hits dealing <5% max health. Immune to chip damage.

**25. Revenge Metabolism** - When hit, gain damage buff for 3s. Aggressive recovery.

**26. Hive Mind Resistance** - Reduced damage from same enemy type. Fifth consecutive hit = near immunity.

### Mobility

**27. Momentum Conservation** - Continuous direction = +50% speed. Rewards commitment.

**28. Gravity Defiance** - Reduced hover cost. 3D battlefield access.

**29. Afterimage Trail** - Dashing spawns damaging afterimages. Speed = offense.

**30. Terrain Memory** - Arena "remembers" paths. Revisiting = speed boost. Routing puzzles.

### Utility

**31. Pheromone Efficiency** - All pheromone abilities +50% duration.

**32. Sensory Expansion** - See attack telegraphs earlier/wider.

**33. Resource Magnetism** - Greater pickup attraction radius.

**34. Critical Consistency** - Lower crit chance, but always exactly 3x damage.

## Enemy Types

### Workers (Fodder)

**35. Forager Bee** - Basic. Moves toward, attacks on contact. One-hit. Satisfying in quantity.

**36. Builder Bee** - Constructs wax barriers blocking movement. Prioritize for maneuverability.

**37. Nurse Bee** - Heals nearby damaged bees. Low HP, high priority target.

**38. Undertaker Bee** - Collects corpses. 5 collected = vengeance swarm. Prevent accumulation.

### Soldiers (Elites)

**39. Guard Bee** - High HP, slow, wide arc. Mobile wall hiding the swarm.

**40. Assassin Bee** - Fast, low HP, high damage. Ignores formations, direct pursuit.

**41. Bomber Bee** - Explodes on death including other bees. Chain reaction risk/reward.

**42. Shield Bee** - Projects barrier blocking projectiles. Must flank or melee.

### Specialists

**43. Pollen Carrier** - Slow, leaves vision-obscuring/slowing cloud trail.

**44. Honey Bomber** - Drops sticky pools rooting temporarily.

**45. Scout Bee** - Doesn't attack. 3s vision = swarm surge. Hide or eliminate fast.

**46. Dancer Bee** - Waggle dance buffs nearby bee damage. Time-pressure target.

---

## Formation Mechanics

**47. Pincer Formation** - Two groups from opposite sides. Time dash through gap.

**48. Spiral Swarm** - Bees orbit central point, tightening. Creates panic.

**49. Wave Pattern** - Lines sweeping across with dodge gaps. Rhythm-game timing.

**50. Shield Wall** - Guards in front, workers behind. Break through or flank.

---

## Environmental Mechanics

**51. Pollen Clouds** - Reduced visibility for both sides. Hide or ambush.

**52. Honey Pools** - 50% movement slow. Affects bees too. Terrain strategy.

**53. Wax Walls** - Destructible cover. Risk blocking your own escape.

**54. Flower Platforms** - Elevated range bonus, but exposed to flyers.

**55. Royal Jelly Fountains** - Periodic healing zones contested by both sides.

**56. Smoke Vents** - Stun all bees temporarily. Limited uses.

---

## Risk/Reward Systems

**57. Graze Bonus** - Near-misses grant points and meter. Incentivizes danger.

**58. Kill Streak Multiplier** - Consecutive kills without damage. Resets on hit.

**59. Overextension Bonus** - Further from center = more resource drops.

**60. Low Health Rage** - Below 25% = 2x damage. Glass cannon viable.

**61. Perfect Clear** - Kill entire wave in 5s = bonus rewards.

**62. Untouched Wave** - No abilities used = resource bonus. Purity challenge.

---

## Resource Systems

**63. Honey Collection** - Primary currency from enemies. Standard economy.

**64. Royal Jelly** - Rare, enables ascension upgrades. Long-term goal.

**65. Pheromone Gauge** - Ability cost. Regenerates slow, kills grant burst.

**66. Stamina System** - Dashes/defense cost stamina. Walking regenerates.

**67. Venom Reserves** - Ranged drains pool, melee replenishes.

**68. Heat Meter** - Aggression builds heat. Max heat = berserk (high damage, no defense).

---

## Special Events

**69. The Beekeeper** - Massive hand scooping bees (and potentially you). Dodge fingers.

**70. Pesticide Cloud** - Toxic zone encroaching from edges. Everyone pushed center.

**71. Flower Bloom** - All plants bloom. Healing zones + resource caches. Harvest window.

**72. Mutant Wave** - 50% larger enemies with new abilities. Harder, better rewards.

**73. Eclipse** - Darkness. Vision reduced to immediate vicinity. Sound-based detection.

**74. Traitor Bee** - One bee joins your side. Escort mission within survival.

**75. Pheromone Confusion** - Bees attack each other for 10s. Watch chaos, collect drops.

**76. Boss Rush** - Three mini-bosses simultaneously. Complete = permanent upgrade.

---

## Synergy Examples

**77. Bleed + Attack Speed** - Serrated Mandibles + rapid attacks = instant max stacks.

**78. Venom + Crowd Control** - Paralysis + Pheromone Bomb = clump, freeze, detonate.

**79. Mobility + Damage Trail** - Afterimage + Momentum = speed becomes DPS.

**80. Low Health + Revenge** - Rage + Revenge = getting hit at low HP is devastating.
