# Monetization & Social Features

> 20 ideas for sustainable business and community building

---

## Monetization Philosophy

> "Following poncle's Vampire Survivors model: substantial content at fair prices, no predatory mechanics."

### Core Principles
1. **No premium currency** - Real money = real content
2. **No pay-to-win** - All gameplay content earnable
3. **No loot boxes** - Know what you're buying
4. **Low price, high value** - $2-5 DLCs with substantial content
5. **Free updates between DLCs** - Build goodwill

## DLC Structure

### Expansion Packs ($2-3 each)

**1. "The Jungle Greenhouse"**
- New biome: Tropical conservatory
- 2 new bee species (Orchid Bee, Stingless Bee)
- 3 new enemy types (Bullet Ant, Tarantula Hawk, Poison Dart Frog)
- 5 new weapons (Orchid Pollen, Vine Whip, Tropical Storm)
- 10 new upgrades
- Hidden boss: The Botanist

**2. "Urban Pollinator"**
- New biome: City rooftops and community gardens
- 2 new species (Mason Bee, Leafcutter Bee)
- 3 new enemies (Pigeon, Rat, Stray Cat)
- Environmental hazards: Traffic, AC units, glass reflections
- Hidden area: The Penthouse Garden

**3. "The Ice Flower"**
- New biome: Arctic greenhouse / frozen tundra
- Hibernation mechanic (slow regeneration while stationary)
- 2 new species (Alpine Bumblebee, Arctic Woolly Bee)
- Ice physics, frozen enemies, avalanche events
- THE BALL becomes "The Blizzard"

**4. "The Underground"**
- New biome: Cave systems and underground hives
- Darkness mechanic (limited visibility)
- Mining resources, crystal enemies
- Bioluminescent flora, echo-location ability

## Social Features

### Sharing & Competition

**11. Crystal Export**
- One-click generate shareable image
- Shows run stats, build path, death screen narrative
- Custom watermark with game branding
- Optimized for Twitter/Discord

**12. Seed Sharing**
- Every run has a seed code
- Share code = identical RNG
- Challenge friends to beat your run
- "Seed of the Week" community event

**13. Ghost Racing**
- Record your movement path
- Others download and race against your ghost
- See exactly where you succeeded/failed
- Leaderboard for "closest to ghost" runs

### Community Features

**14. Global Stats Dashboard**
- Total bees killed worldwide (live counter)
- Most popular species/builds
- Average survival time
- "Colony Status" - are players winning or losing overall?

**15. Build Sharing**
- Export upgrade path as shareable code
- Import to see exactly what someone else ran
- "Top Builds This Week" showcase
- Reddit/Discord integration

**16. Weekly Challenges**
- Curated modifiers each week
- Global leaderboard
- Cosmetic rewards for participation
- "Champion of the Week" title

## Pricing Summary

| Item | Price | Content |
|------|-------|---------|
| Base Game | $5-10 | Full game, 8+ species, 50+ upgrades |
| Expansion DLC | $2-3 | New biome, 2 species, 3 enemies, 5 weapons |
| Skin Pack | $1-2 | 4-6 cosmetic skins |
| Trail Effect | $0.99 | Single trail effect |
| Soundtrack | $3-5 | Full OST download |

### Revenue Philosophy
- Low friction purchases
- High perceived value
- Regular content = regular purchases
- Happy players = word of mouth

---

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

1. **No season passes** - Buy what you want, when you want
2. **No FOMO tactics** - Limited items return eventually
3. **No energy systems** - Play as much as you want
4. **No gacha mechanics** - Know what you're getting
5. **No pay-to-skip** - Earning is the fun part
6. **No ads** - Clean premium experience

---

*"Build a game people love. Charge fairly for extra content. Respect your players."*
