# Meta-Game & Progression Brainstorm

> 50 unlockables, trophies, relics, and replayability features

---

## Unlockable Characters (Hornet Species)

### Starter
**1. Vespa mandarinia** (Giant Asian Hornet) - "The Executioner"
- Baseline stats, all-rounder. Unlocked by default.

### Species to Unlock

**2. Vespa velutina** (Yellow-Legged) - "The Ghost"
- -20% damage, +40% speed, shorter dash cooldown
- *Unlock*: Escape THE BALL 5 times in one run
- *Playstyle*: Hit-and-run

**3. Vespa crabro** (European) - "The Tank"
- +50% health, -25% speed, attacks push back
- *Unlock*: Survive 3 minutes with Coordination above 8
- *Playstyle*: Brawler

**4. Vespa tropica** (Greater Banded) - "The Berserker"
- +30% damage below 50% health, no i-frames on hit
- *Unlock*: Kill 300 bees in one run
- *Playstyle*: High-risk aggression

**5. Vespa affinis** (Lesser Banded) - "The Diplomat"
- Kills delay coordination by 0.5s each. -15% damage.
- *Unlock*: Win with 0 balls formed
- *Playstyle*: Prevent THE BALL through pressure

**6. Vespa luctuosa** (Philippine) - "The Shadow"
- Invisible while stationary (bees lose track). Cannot attack invisible.
- *Unlock*: Complete run using only Assassin archetype
- *Playstyle*: Stealth

**7. Vespa soror** (Black Shield) - "The Martyr"
- Death creates massive explosion. Respawn once if kills > 100.
- *Unlock*: Die to THE BALL at wave 10+ with 200+ kills
- *Playstyle*: Death explosion builds

**8. Vespa analis** (Yellow Vented) - "The Swarm"
- Summon 2 weak ally hornets every 30s. -40% personal damage.
- *Unlock*: Kill 50 enhanced bees total
- *Playstyle*: Pack hunting

## Run Modifiers

### Curses (Harder, More Rewards)
**37. Curse of the Queen** - Royals spawn 50% more. 2x XP from royals.
**38. Curse of Memory** - Start at coordination level 3. +25% relic progress.
**39. Curse of Silence** - No upgrade choices (random auto-select). Guaranteed relic fragment.
**40. Curse of the Clock** - THE BALL forms 30% faster. Speed Run leaderboard.

### Blessings (Easier)
**41. Blessing of the Swarm** - Start with 2 random upgrades. No trophy progress.
**42. Blessing of Vision** - All gaps permanently visible. 50% XP gain.

### Challenge Modifiers
**43. "Glass Cannon"** - 3x damage, 1 HP. Special leaderboard.
**44. "Eternal Ball"** - THE BALL never fully dissipates (reforms at 50%).
**45. "Mirror Match"** - Bees also have upgrade effects.
