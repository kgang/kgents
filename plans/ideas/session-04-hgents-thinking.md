## Key Insights

### H-gents Are System Therapy
Not user therapy. H-gents examine the **agent system itself**:
- What does our code repress? (Jung)
- Where do requirements contradict? (Hegel)
- What can't we represent? (Lacan)

### Shadow ‚â† Evil
The shadow is **constitutive**. No identity without exclusion.
- "I am helpful" ‚Üí shadows "capacity to harm"
- "I am safe" ‚Üí shadows "latent capabilities"
- Integration, not elimination.

### Registers Are Not Just Categories
They're **knotted together** (Borromean). When one slips, the whole system loosens:
- High Imaginary + Low Symbolic = Wishful thinking
- High Real proximity + Low Symbolic = Unstructured chaos
- Knot status matters.

### Metaphor Is Computation
