Based on Kent's examples and maximum demonstration potential:

| 1 | **"I Learned to Write a Children's Show"** | Screenwriting + character design | Tangible output (pilot script + visuals), emotionally engaging |

| 3 | **"I Learned to Design a Board Game"** | Game design + systems thinking | Physical prototype possible, community playtesting |
| 4 | **"I Learned to Produce a Podcast"** | Audio production + research | Meta (about the channel journey?), practical |
| 5 | **"I Learned to Make a Short Film"** | Screenplay + storyboarding | Cinematic, high production value demo |

### Episode Structure (15-25 minutes)

```
1. THE CHALLENGE (1-2 min)
   "I've never written for children. Can I create a show pitch?"

2. THE PLAN (2-3 min)
   "Here's my agent setup: character agent, plot agent, dialogue agent..."

3. THE STRUGGLE (5-8 min)
   Real attempts, failures, iterations
   "The first output was... educational. In the bad way."

4. THE BREAKTHROUGH (3-5 min)
   The moment it starts working
   Show the actual agent interactions

5. THE OUTPUT (3-5 min)
   Present the creative artifact seriously
   Not "AI made this" but "I made this with AI"

6. THE REFLECTION (2-3 min)
   What did I learn? What's the limit?
   Honest quality assessment vs. traditional craft
```

## Risk Mitigation

### Known Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| "AI art isn't real art" backlash | Frame as augmentation, not replacement; interview traditional artists |
| Output quality concerns | Be honest about limitations; show failures |
| Burnout from high production | 1-2/month pace; batch production |
| Algorithm changes | Multi-platform from start; build email list |
| "Just another AI channel" perception | Emphasize agent-building, not tool-using |

### The Integrity Line

**Always**:
- Show the human in the loop
- Acknowledge what AI can't do
- Be honest about quality vs. traditional craft
- Celebrate both AI and human creativity

**Never**:
- Claim AI equals trained human craft
- Dismiss the value of traditional training
- Oversell capabilities
- Hide failures
